## üéØ VATS System

### How to Use VATS
1. **Press V** to activate VATS
2. Time slows to 25% speed
3. **TAB** to cycle through targets
4. View hit % chance based on:
   - Distance (closer = better)
   - Perception (better detection)
   - Agility (better targeting)
5. **SPACE** to fire normal shot
6. **C** to fire Critical (if ready)
7. **V** to exit VATS

### VATS Mechanics
- **Stamina Cost**: 20 stamina per use, 10 per shot
- **Hit Chance**: 95% base - distance penalty + Perception/Agility bonuses
- **Critical Meter**: Fills with each hit (10 + Luck√ó2 per hit)
- **Critical Storage**: Store crits with Critical Banker perk
- **Slow Motion**: 25% time scale during targeting

### VATS Perks
- **Concentrated Fire**: +20% damage per consecutive hit on same target
- **Penetrator**: Shots ignore 50% armor
- **VATS Enhanced Legendary**: -25% stamina cost

## üéÆ How It Works in Arma 3

### Character Creation
1. **Start**: All S.P.E.C.I.AL. at 1
2. **Level Up**: Gain XP from kills, contracts, sieges
3. **Distribute Points**: Increase S.P.E.C.I.AL. or buy perks
4. **Specialize**: Focus on gun types (rifles, automatic, pistols, heavy)

### XM8 Interface
- View S.P.E.C.I.AL. stats
- Browse available perks (organized by attribute)
- Allocate perk points
- View current build
- See perk requirements

### Combat Flow
1. **Engage Enemy**: Use your specialized weapon type
2. **Apply Perks**: Rifleman, Commando, etc. boost damage
3. **Use VATS**: Press V for tactical targeting
4. **Build Crits**: Fill critical meter with hits
5. **Execute Critical**: Massive bonus damage
6. **Loot**: Chance for legendary weapons

### Progression Example
**Level 1-10**: Focus on combat perks (Rifleman, Bloody Mess)
**Level 11-20**: Add crafting (Gun Nut, Armorer)
**Level 21-30**: Maximize critical hits (Better Criticals, Critical Banker)
**Level 31-40**: Advanced perks (Penetrator, Concentrated Fire)
